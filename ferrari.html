Ferrari

    Ferrari does not allow any modifications to their car, even if they’re the technical owner of the car
    -Includes customizations, paint jobs and tampering with the Ferrari’s logo on the car.

    The owner of a Ferrari is not allowed to sell the car without notifying Ferrari or before a year of ownership

    Ferrari forbids pink from the branding

Violating any of the guidelines punishes the owner with a lifetime ban from Ferrari or in the worst case, a lawsuit from Ferrari. Also, employees of Ferrari are not allowed to buy a Ferrari at all.

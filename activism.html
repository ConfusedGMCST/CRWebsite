Activism

    The most popular form of activism is boycotting Boycotting is the act of an organized group of people refusing to do service with a certain product, organization or person

    Media attention online can also be used to promote consumer activism
    The internet allows a wide range of audiences to view activist beliefs which can potentially lead up to certain companies viewing the backlash from the media
